## About the School  
Upgraded to a full secondary school, PM Shri Govt Secondary School provides co-educational learning from Classes 1 to 10. Our strength has grown from ~350 students to over 855 today. We provide a state-of-the-art smart classroom, ICT lab, free coaching for Navodaya and Neterhat scholarships, and emphasize holistic growth.
